📘 PROJECT DEFINITION REPORT (PDR)
Project Title

Employee Leave Management System

1. Project Overview

The Employee Leave Management System is a role-based software application designed to automate and manage employee leave requests, approvals, and reporting processes within an organization. The system replaces traditional manual methods such as emails, spreadsheets, and paper forms with a centralized, secure, and efficient digital platform.

The application supports multiple user roles including Employee, Manager, and HR/Admin, ensuring controlled access, transparent workflows, and accurate leave tracking.

2. Problem Statement

Many organizations still rely on manual or semi-automated leave management processes, which result in inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, delayed approvals, and reporting challenges. Employees often lack visibility into their leave status, managers struggle to track pending requests, and HR teams face difficulties generating accurate monthly or annual reports.

As organizations grow in size and adopt hybrid or remote work models, these issues become more critical, leading to operational delays, payroll errors, and employee dissatisfaction.

3. Causes of the Problem

The key causes contributing to inefficient leave management are:

Manual Processes

Use of emails, spreadsheets, and paper-based forms

High risk of errors and data loss

Lack of Centralized System

Leave data scattered across multiple files

No single source of truth

No Role-Based Access Control

Employees, managers, and HR accessing shared files

Security and authorization issues

Poor Reporting Mechanism

Manual preparation of HR reports

Time-consuming and error-prone

Scalability Issues

Existing methods fail as employee count increases

4. Purpose of the Project

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a centralized, automated, and role-based leave management system that simplifies leave workflows, ensures data accuracy, and improves HR operational efficiency.
